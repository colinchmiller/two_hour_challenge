Spider Plant

The spider plant (Chlorophytum comosum), also known as the airplane plant, is an herb native to tropical and southern Africa, but has since spread to other regions. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of growing and decorative, variegated leaves. An excellent air cleaner, particularly for formaldehyde, this plant requires consistent sunlight but is resistant to the effects of infrequent watering. It is also an easy plant to reproduce, as it gives off smaller plantlets that can be easily transferred to new pots.
