Areca Palm

A palm of many names, Dypsis lutescens is also known as the areca palm, bamboo palm, golden can palm, yellow palm, or butterfly palm. It is native to the island of Madagascar, but has since been spread by humans throughout Central and South America, as well as the Caribbean. Arguably the best air cleaner in the world, it is also beneficial in the home as a humidifier, respirating water vapor into the air at a substantial rate. As a result, this plant requires frequent watering. It also benefits from a great deal of sunlight.
